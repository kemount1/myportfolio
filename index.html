My Approach

"Technology is an art. I see coding and creativity as deeply connected, bridging beauty and function in service of human needs."

I don’t want to design things that are merely useful, but things that are beautiful, reflecting the way God created the world to not just be useful, but full of beauty, delight, and connection.

My background in education and global missions has shaped how I design for how people think, learn, and stay engaged. Whether working with physical assets, digital tools, or applications, I see them as ways to meet people where they are.
