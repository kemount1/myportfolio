My Approach

"I bridge the gap between user intuition and business ambition."

While my work is rooted in high-level design and aesthetic excellence, my primary value lies in Strategic Advisory. I serve as a liaison between the user and the product—clarifying vision, aligning marketing with company goals, and ensuring every design choice serves a greater objective.

I understand people as much as I understand pixels. My background in education and global missions has shaped my ability to listen for unmet needs and translate them into functional product roadmaps. I don't just create; I solve for the connection between product, brand, and the human beings they serve.
